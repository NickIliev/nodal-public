# Nodal — Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your Nodal account and all associated data at any time.

## In-app (recommended, instant)

1. Open the Nodal app
2. Navigate to the Profile screen
3. Scroll to the bottom
4. Tap **Delete account** and confirm deletion

This permanently removes your Firebase Auth account, your Firestore profile (username, birth date, birth place, conversations), and any locally cached data on the device. Irreversible.

## Email request (if you can no longer access the app)

Email **iliev.nick007@gmail.com** with:

- Subject: `Nodal account deletion request.`
- The email address registered on your account

We'll process the request within 7 business days and confirm when complete.
